Everyday care

General Dentistry

The everyday care that keeps small problems from becoming big, worrying ones — delivered with a gentle touch, and included as standard on every membership plan.

  • Check-ups & examinations — thorough, unhurried reviews of your teeth, gums and jaw, including a full oral health and cancer screening every time.

Good to know

Common questions

Do you accept new patients? +

Yes — new members are always welcome, whatever your age, including anyone transferring from another practice. Getting started is as simple as choosing a plan and booking your first appointment.

How do the membership plans work? +

Every patient joins one of three plans — Silver, Gold or Platinum — billed simply each month. Your plan covers routine check-ups and hygiene visits as standard, plus a fixed discount on any further treatment, so there's never a surprise invoice.

Can I book a one-off appointment without joining a plan? +

No — we're a plan-only practice, so every patient (including emergency visits) needs an active membership. It's what lets us keep pricing predictable and appointments unhurried for everyone. Joining takes a few minutes — see our plans or get in touch and we'll talk you through it.

I'm nervous about the dentist — can you help? +

Absolutely. Let us know when you book and we'll adjust pacing, explain every step in advance, and give you full control to pause at any time.

Is there parking near the practice? +

There's no on-site car park, but there's convenient short-stay and public parking around Tuesday Market Place, just a short walk from the practice.
