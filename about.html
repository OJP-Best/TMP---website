About us

A practice built on trust, one appointment at a time

We're a small, independent, plan-only practice based on Tuesday Market Place — the same square that's anchored King's Lynn's daily life for centuries. We think dental care should feel the same way: familiar, warm, and like part of the family.

Our home on Tuesday Market Place since 2005

The practice occupies a Grade II listed Georgian building overlooking Tuesday Market Place. Above the door is the imprint known locally as the "Witch's Heart" — a well-known King's Lynn landmark and one of the town's most visited points of local history.

Our story

Founded on plain-speaking, patient-first dentistry

When Tuesday Market Dental Practice opened its doors in 2005, the idea behind it was simple: too many people put off going to the dentist because appointments felt rushed, clinical, or difficult to understand — or because pricing felt unpredictable. We wanted something different: a practice where you're treated like a person, not a patient number. Every member joins a simple monthly plan, every treatment is explained in plain English, and you're always given the time you need to feel comfortable and informed.

Today we're proud to look after many members across the Norfolk region — from nervous first-timers to families who've been coming to us for years, and who we've come to know and care about.

How we work

What you can expect from us

Unhurried appointments

We build real time into every slot, so questions get answered properly and you never feel like just another name on the list.

A practice that listens

Practice owner

Led by Dr. David Best

Dr. David Best

Practice Owner & Lead Dentist, BDS (Hons) (Lon), LDS RCS (Eng), DGDP (UK)

David opened Tuesday Market Dental Practice in 2005 because he genuinely believes patients deserve better: considered, unhurried dentistry from a team who care, through straightforward membership plans rather than confusing price lists. With over two decades of clinical experience, he personally oversees every treatment plan and still sees his own patients — with a particular soft spot for nervous first-timers and a strong belief that prevention and a caring chairside manner go hand in hand.

There's genuinely no limit to what David can do for your smile — from routine check-ups and hygiene through to more advanced procedures like dental implants, crowns and full smile reconstructions, all delivered with the same unhurried, personal approach.
